INDIAN PREMIERE LEAGUE (2021)

IPL

The Indian Premier League (IPL) is a professional Twenty20 cricket league, contested by eight teams based out of eight different Indian cities. The league was founded by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) in 2007. It is usually held between March and May of every year. IPL 2021 is the 14th Season in the IPL History. At present, there are 8 teams.

IPL TEAMS 2021

Chennai Super Kings

CSK

The Chennai Super Kings (CSK) are a franchise cricket team based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. They play in the Indian Premier League (IPL). The team is captained by Mahendra Singh Dhoni and coached by Stephen Fleming.

Royal Challengers Banglore

RCB

The Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) are a franchise cricket team based in Bangalore, Karnataka, that plays in the Indian Premier League (IPL). The team is captained by Virat Kohli and coached by Simon Katich.

Sunrisers Hyderabad

SRH

The Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH) are a franchise cricket team based in Hyderabad, Telangana, India, that plays in the Indian Premier League (IPL). The team is captained by Kane Williamson and coached by Trevor Bayliss.

Mumbai Indians

MI

The Mumbai Indians (MI) are a franchise cricket team based in Mumbai, Maharashtra, that plays in the Indian Premier League (IPL). The team is captained by Rohit Sharma and coached by Mahela Jayawardene.

Kolkata Knight Riders

KKR

The Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) are a franchise cricket team based in Kolkata, West Bengal, that plays in the Indian Premier League (IPL). The team is captained by Eoin Morgan and coached by Brendon McCullum.

Rajasthan Royals

RR

The Rajasthan Royals (RR) are a franchise cricket team based in Jaipur, Rajasthan, that plays in the Indian Premier League (IPL). The team is captained by Sanju Samson and coached by Trevor Penney.

Delhi Capitals

DC

The Delhi Capitals (DC) are a franchise cricket team based in Delhi, India, that plays in the Indian Premier League (IPL). The team is captained by Rishabh Pant and coached by Ricky Ponting.

Punjab Kings

PBKS

The Punjab Kings (PBKS) are a franchise cricket team based in Bangalore, Karnataka, that plays in the Indian Premier League (IPL). The team is captained by K.L Rahul and coached by Anil Kumble.
